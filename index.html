E-BIPMS - Electronic Barangay Integrated Profiling and Monitoring System for Brgy. Kanlurang Bukal

This project aims to improve the traditional mode of delivering services and manage the barangay records and data efficiently and effectively. The project aims to address specific problems such as inefficient record-keeping, lack of transparency, limited resources, and inadequate data analysis faced by small barangays like Brgy. Kanlurang Bukal. By implementing an E-BIPMS system, the barangay hopes to streamline operations, reduce costs, enhance transparency, and improve decision-making related to programs and services. Additionally, the project seeks to surmount financial and personnel obstacles that can hinder efficient data management through operational simplification and cost reduction. Overall, the project aims to address the specific problems faced by Brgy. Kanlurang Bukal and improve the quality of services delivered to its residents.
